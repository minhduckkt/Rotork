ELC Range

The ELC 100 electric linear actuator delivers a perfect balance of performance and versatility in a compact form. This actuator delivers 1.0 kN of thrust with a 20 mm stroke, selectable actuation speeds (12, 9*, 4, 1.9 s/mm), and weighs 2.5 kg. It is IP54 rated.
